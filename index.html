About me

My name is Symbat Maydanova and I have been working as a photographer for almost a year. Despite the fact that this is a fairly short time, I have a good amount of work. I have worked with various fashion houses such as Yohji Yamamoto, Donna Karan, Vera Wang, Roberto Cavalli. I had contracts with world magazines such as Vogue, Glamour. I also recently had an exhibition in St. Petersburg with my photos. I always strive to develop, not to stand in one place and try to be better than now. I am very hardworking, responsible, punctual, sociable and it is a pleasure for me to work with different people
